# **App Name**: C-Chain Angola

## Core Features:

- Secure User Onboarding & Profiles: Enable users (Producers, Transporters, Buyers) to register with commercial information (Name, NIF, Province), document upload, and an simulated approval process to join the network.
- Dynamic Market Feed: Display real-time logistical and agricultural opportunities (posts) with key details like title, location, value, category, urgency, image, and user information.
- Opportunity Creation & Management: Allow authenticated users to create and publish new posts (opportunities or requests), specifying comprehensive details such as title, description, category, estimated value, location, and urgency.
- Direct Negotiation & Messaging: Provide a private chat interface for users to initiate and manage conversations regarding specific opportunities or general inquiries with other network participants.
- AI-Powered Logistic Risk Assessment Tool: An integrated AI model (Sentinel IA) that analyzes the details of a market post to provide automated insights or identify potential logistical risks.
- Provincial Market Overview: Visually showcase active market participation or available opportunities aggregated by different Angolan provinces, offering a geographical overview.
- Advanced Search & Filtering: Implement robust search functionality and filter options (by category, province, urgency) to help users efficiently find relevant market opportunities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color (for interactive elements, major headings): A deep, professional blue-grey for reliability and structure. HEX: #2D343C (HSL: 220, 15%, 20%).
- Background color (general UI background): A very light blue-grey for a clean and spacious feel. HEX: #F6F8FB (HSL: 220, 10%, 97%).
- Accent color (for highlights and calls to action, distinct from urgency): A vibrant purple to signify innovation and a modern edge. HEX: #924DC9 (HSL: 250, 70%, 60%). The existing red color (#DC2626) will be used for specific urgency indicators and primary CTAs due to its strong visual impact.
- Headline and short text font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-forward, and impactful aesthetic, fitting the blockchain and logistics context. Body text and longer descriptions will use 'Inter' (sans-serif) for optimal readability and a neutral tone.
- Utilize crisp, vector-based line icons from the 'lucide-react' library for clarity and modern consistency across all interface elements.
- Clean and organized layout featuring a sticky header for navigation, a prominent central feed for content, and responsive sidebars (collapsible on smaller screens) for supplementary information like AI insights and network suggestions.
- Incorporate subtle animations and transitions for modals, loading states, and interactive elements to provide a smooth and engaging user experience, reflecting active participation and network dynamics.
